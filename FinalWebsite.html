About Me

Hello there! My name is Lara!

I am currently 23 years YOUNG. My birthday is on March 10, 1995. I was born in the Philippines and moved to Canada when I was only 4 years old. I am VERY short. My family consists of my dad, my mom, my two older sisters, my brother, and my doggy, Timbit! I have been living in Mississauga my entire life and I LOVE it. I am a FOURTH year (yay) Brock University student majoring in Medical Science - it's hard, I don't recommend it. However, since going to Brock Univiersity, I have been living in St. Catharines during the school year (and I hate it), but I do go back to my happy place every weekend and during the summer! I spend the majority of my summers working at a medical company, Mui Scientific. I hope that in the near future, I could take everything I have learned from working there and move on to bigger and better things! (fingers crossed!)

Three things I enjoy doing most include: shopping, (indoor) rock climbing, and watching Youtube videos. Some Youtubers I enjoy watching include: Zoella, ACE Family, JK Party, and many more. You should definitely check them out as well on Youtube!
