It's All About Me!

Woof! Woof!

Hi! My name is Timbit Oliveros!

I know my name is Timbit, but my family doesn't ever call me that unless I am in big trouble... Instead, I have lots of nicknames. They like to call me, "Tb", "Timbs", "Timby", "Ba", "Teebs", "Bumbum", and so much more! I know I have a lot of nicknames, but I respond to all of them. I am cute, smart, cuddly, energetic, and loving, but most importantly, I am the KING OF THE WORLD! I am the Oliveros family's favourite family member. I am a Yorkshire Terrier (Yorkie for short). I am 8 years old and my birthday is on August 18, 2010! I am about 8 pounds, but it always changes depending on the treaties I eat! I also love the colour red. When I was just a puppy, my family always dressed me up in blue, but now I am ALL red. I am very spoiled. My whole family spoils me... But I'm still a good boy!

I am mostly an indoor doggy, but I love going on walks and playing outside in my backyard. Sometimes, the grass is even taller than I am! My favourite thing to do is go on walks with my family and eat ice cream. They always take me to an icecream shop in Streetsville called "Murphys" and it is so delicious. My parents don't take me out for walks or let me out in the backyard anymore though because there are four coyotes living across my house... Don't they know I am the king of the world?

Look at all my cute pictures from when I was just a couple months old to now!
